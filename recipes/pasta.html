Amatriciana pasta

One of my favorite pasta dishes. The bacon and red pepper make this dish special

Ingredients

  • 4 slices diced bacon
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 teasoon crushed red pepper
  • 2 cans stewed tomatoes
  • 1 pound linguine pasta
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
  • parmesan cheese (to taste)

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon in a saucepan over medium heat
  2. Keep some leftover bacon fat in the saucepan and mix in the onions and cook for several minutes. Stir in the garlic and red pepper flakes for 30 seconds.
  3. Add canned tomates, undrained, and simmer for 10 minutes. Make sure to break up the tomatoes as it cooks.
  4. While the sauce is simmering, cook the pasta until al dente.
  5. Stir basil in sauce, then toss with the cooked pasta. Finish by adding paremsan cheese.
