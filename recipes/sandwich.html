The best PB&J sandwich

Such a simple recipe, but never fails to be delicious

Ingredients

  • Your favorite bread (preferably buttermilk or other white bread)
  • Your favorite peanut butter
  • your favorite jelly flavor

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one of the slices of bread generously.
  2. Spread the jelly on the other slice of bread.
  3. Put the bread slices together.
  4. Enjoy!
