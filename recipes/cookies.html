chocolate chip cookies

This is a recipe for my favorite chocolate chip cookies

These cookies took a lot of time and effort to get the ingredients just right. They are on the buttery side, and you can easily swap out or combine both light and dark brown sugar. I love the taste of the mollases, so I opt for dark brown

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar (light or brown, or combination of both
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Combine sugar and salt a large bowl
  2. Add melted butter and egg and stir until mixed well
  3. Add vanilla extract and mix in
  4. Add the flour and baking powder and mix well
  5. Add in choclate chips and
  6. Put the dough in the fridge and let sit for at least 2 hours and up to 24 hours
  7. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  8. Roll into tablespoon sized balls and bake for 10 - 12 minutes
  9. Remove from over and let cool for 30 minutes
  10. Enjoy!
